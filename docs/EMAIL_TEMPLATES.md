## 2. Pilot Welcome / Onboarding

**Subject:** Welcome to BONDING! Here's how to start

**Body:**

Welcome to BONDING, {NAME}!

You're now part of the hyperlocal pilot. Here's your 5-minute setup guide:

**Step 1: Open the app**
Go to https://bonding-meatspace.pages.dev on your phone.
Tap "Join the Reaction" and then "Demo" to see it in action.

**Step 2: Create your identity**
The first time you open the mobile app, it generates a cryptographic key pair on your device. No account, no password — you are a unique atom.

**Step 3: Check in**
When you're at a cafe, park, or shared space, open the app and check in. The app shares only a geohash (~5km precision) — your exact location stays private.

**Step 4: Ping and bond**
See who else is nearby. Send a ping. If they accept, a bond forms.

**Step 5: React and evolve**
Log reactions (problem_solved, resource_shared) to earn valence and advance your community's era.

**Need help?** Reply to this email or check the FAQ on the site.

— The BONDING team
