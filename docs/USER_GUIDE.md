# USER_GUIDE.md — How to Play

## Welcome to BONDING

You are an **atom**. Your mission: form **bonds** with other atoms, collaborate on **reactions**, and build real‑world connections.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Open the app** on your phone (or browser).
2. On first launch, the app generates a **cryptographic key pair** — this is your identity. No email, no password.
3. Set up your profile: choose a display name, list your skills and interests.

---

## The Map

You'll see a map with **five zones**:

- **🌿 Calm** — parks, libraries, quiet spaces.
- **🔬 Lab** — makerspaces, coworking, workshops.
- **🍳 Kitchen** — cafes, restaurants, community kitchens.
- **🌊 Deep** — retreats, nature spots for deep conversation.
- **🌀 Wild** — adventure, exploration, serendipity.

Tap a zone to **select** it.

---

## Check In

After selecting a zone, press the **"Check In"** button. This records your presence (as a geohash prefix) and updates your status.

---

## Ping (Invite)

To connect with someone in the same zone, **send a ping**. If they accept, a **bond** is formed.

---

## Bond & React

Once bonded, you can **check in together** more often. Complete a **reaction** together: _"We helped carry groceries"_ or _"We built a bookshelf"_. Reactions are logged and count toward your shared history.

---

## Valence (Trust)

Your **Valence** is a hidden score (0.1–2.0). It increases when you show up and bond. It decreases if you flake or reject too many pings. Higher valence means more matching opportunities. (Valence is private — you never see it, but the algorithm does.)

---

## Profile

View your stats: bonds, check‑ins, current zone. See your list of bonds and your shared history.

---

## Privacy

- Your **exact location is never sent to the server** — only a geohash prefix (~5km precision).
- Your identity is tied to a key on your device; lose the key, lose the account (privacy reset).
- All connections are **mutual opt‑in**.

---

## Success

BONDING succeeds when you:

- **Make a new friend**.
- **Help someone with a task**.
- **Discover a new place in your neighborhood**.
- **Build a community** that meets offline, without the app.
