# MARKETING.md — Positioning & Messaging

## Elevator Pitch

**BONDING** is a real‑world game that turns your neighborhood into a chemistry lab. Humans are atoms, relationships are bonds, and solving problems together is the reaction. No streaks, no points, no surveillance — just a gentle nudge to meet your people.

---

## Value Proposition

- **For individuals:** Finally, an excuse to get out and meet people who share your interests and complement your skills.
- **For communities:** A tool to strengthen local ties, reduce loneliness, and turn passive residents into active citizens.
- **For neighborhoods:** Hyperlocal connections that lead to real‑world help, collaboration, and trust.

---

## Key Messages

| Audience                 | Message                                                      |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Lonely urbanites**     | "Same bowl, same room — your people first."                  |
| **Creators & makers**    | "Find your lab partner. Build something real."               |
| **Community organizers** | "Turn your neighborhood into a living molecule."             |
| **Privacy‑conscious**    | "We don't track you. Your location stays with you."          |
| **Ethical designers**    | "No engagement loops. No variable rewards. Just connection." |

---

## Brand Voice

- **Calm, warm, intentional.**
- **Inviting, not demanding.**
- **Clear about boundaries: we are not a dating app, not a social network, not a gamification machine.**

---

## Call to Action

- **"Join the reaction."**
- **"Check in. Ping. Bond. Solve."**
- **"Your neighborhood is a molecule."**
