## 1. Executive Summary (150 words)

P31 Labs, Inc. requests **$20,000** to launch Phase 1 of **Neuro-Tech Career Catalyst**: a 6-month bootcamp and project lab for 12 neurodivergent young adults (ages 18–30) in Camden County, GA. Participants train in open-source software development, Raspberry Pi edge computing, and human-computer interaction, building functional assistive-tech prototypes (e.g., QuantumCalm) using our K₄-based framework.

This phase delivers a nationally replicable curriculum and open-source asset library. Year 2 (internship placement) will be funded by subsequent grants. The program addresses ~85% neurodivergent unemployment/underemployment and Georgia's growing tech workforce gap.

## 3. Problem Statement

- Neurodivergent individuals (autism, ADHD, dyslexia) possess exceptional pattern-recognition, logic, and focus — ideal for software engineering.
- Traditional hiring processes filter them out (resume gaps, non-standard interviews, lack of accommodations).
- Existing bootcamps (TechBridge, CodePath) lack neuro-affirming curricula or mentorship.

**Target:** 12 young adults in Camden County and surrounding areas who are unemployed or underemployed with demonstrated interest in technology.

---

## 4. Proposed Solution — Phase 1 Only ($20,000)

**Neuro-Tech Career Catalyst — Phase 1 (Months 1–6)**

1. **Weeks 1–8 (Bootcamp):** Python, Git, Docker, Raspberry Pi OS, basic electronics. Neurodivergent instructors, flexible pacing, sensory-friendly spaces.
2. **Weeks 9–24 (Project Lab):** Participants build assistive-tech devices (AAC interfaces, environmental controls, sensory-regulation tools) using the K₄-based framework and open-source stack.

**Phase 2 (Year 2 — NOT included in this request):** Paid internships with partner companies, funded by subsequent grants (e.g., Spectrum Digital Education, AWS Imagine).

**Key differentiators:**

- Neurodivergent-led design
- No student debt — free to participants
- Open-source assets — all curricula, code, and hardware designs released under open license
- Nationally replicable — deployable by Sensata employees in MA, CA, VT, MI, ID, VA, MN
