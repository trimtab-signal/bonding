## Filing Instructions

### 1. Online (recommended — instant confirmation)

Go to [https://ecorp.sos.ga.gov](https://ecorp.sos.ga.gov)  
Log in → Annual Registrations → File Annual Registration  
Select: **First Annual Registration** (within 90 days of formation)  
Pay $40 fee with credit card  
Save the **filed stamp** + payment receipt immediately

### 2. Mail (if preferred)

Download form from [https://sos.ga.gov/annual-registration](https://sos.ga.gov/annual-registration)  
Mail check for $40 + form to:  
> Georgia Secretary of State  
> Corporations Division  
> 2 MLK Jr. Dr. SE, Suite 313 West Tower  
> Atlanta, GA 30334-1530

**Note:** Mail filing takes 2–4 weeks for confirmation. Online is strongly recommended given the July 2 deadline.

## Notes

- The **Certificate of Incorporation** (filed 04/03/2026) is the **formation document** — it is NOT the Annual Registration.
- The **Annual Registration** is a **separate, recurring compliance filing** required to maintain good standing.
- If you miss the July 2 deadline, file immediately — the $400 late fee is cheaper than administrative dissolution (which typically occurs between July–September).
- Administrative dissolution strips you of liability protection and the right to use the business name.
- Keep the filed document in your corporate records binder (digital is fine).
