# BONDING — Store Listings

## Apple App Store

### App Name

BONDING — Real Connection, Real World

### Subtitle (30 chars max)

Find your people. IRL.

### Description (4000 chars max)

**Real connection doesn't happen on a screen.**

BONDING is a private, location-aware app that helps you discover and connect with people near you — without surveillance, algorithms, or engagement loops.

**How it works:**

- Check in when you're at a café, park, or coworking space.
- See who else is nearby — by time zone, not coordinates.
- Send a Ping to say "I'm open to connecting."
- Earn bonds through real IRL encounters.
- Build your local meatspace — starting with people who share your patch.

**Privacy by design:**

- No accounts. No email required.
- Geohash-only location — your precise location never leaves your device.
- Ephemeral check-ins with automatic expiry.
- Open source under MIT. Auditable by anyone.

**No streaks. No leaderboards. No engagement loops.**

BONDING is calm technology. It works when you need it, and fades into the background when you don't.

Built for people who want to reclaim real-world connection — one check-in at a time.

---

**Note:** BONDING is currently in pilot. You may encounter empty zones as the community grows. Check in anyway — you'll help map the meatspace.
